Background

Understanding Bias in Large Language Models

Introduction to AI Bias

Large Language Models are trained on vast amounts of data from the internet. This data contains human biases and historical inequalities that the models learn and reproduce. Our project investigates how these biases manifest in image-generating LLMs when asked to create photographs of people in common occupations.

This section provides background information on what biases are, why fairness in AI matters, and why we chose to focus on gender and race in our investigation.
